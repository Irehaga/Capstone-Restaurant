Our Specialty

Bun Bo Hue: A Spicy Vietnamese Delight

Discover the bold flavors of Bun Bo Hue, an authentic Vietnamese soup known for its spicy and aromatic broth. This delectable dish features tender beef, pork, and rice vermicelli, simmered in a lemongrass-infused broth with a hint of chili. Topped with fresh herbs, it's a perfect balance of spicy, savory, and refreshing notes, making each bowl a vibrant culinary experience.
